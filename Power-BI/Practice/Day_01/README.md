# Power BI Practice - Day 01

## Dataset

- **File:** P6-SuperStoreUS-2015.xlsx (Excel)
- **Sheets:** Orders, Returns, Users
- **Key Columns:** Order Date, Region, Sales, Profit, Customer Segment, Product Category, Ship Mode, State or Province, City

---

## What I Did

- Loaded the SuperStore Excel file into Power BI
- Used the Orders sheet as the main data source
- Created a Bar Chart to show Monthly Sales across all Regions in 2015
- Grouped the months by Quarter (Qtr 1, Qtr 2) on the X-axis
- Compared sales figures for 4 regions side by side

---

## Visual Created

**Chart Type:** Clustered Bar Chart

**Title:** Monthly Sales Across Regions in 2015

**X-axis:** Quarter - Month (January to June)

**Y-axis:** Sales Figure (Thousands)

**Legend:** Central, East, South, West

---

## What I Learned

- How to connect and load an Excel file into Power BI
- How to build a clustered bar chart
- How to group dates by Quarter and Month
- How to use region-based color legends to compare data
